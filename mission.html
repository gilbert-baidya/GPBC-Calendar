Our Mission

Passion for God, Compassion for People

Our Beginning

Grace and Praise Bangladeshi Church was founded with a vision to create a spiritual home for the Bangladeshi community in California. Our journey began with a small group of believers who gathered to worship God and celebrate their cultural heritage.

Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community that embraces both our Bangladeshi roots and our identity as followers of Christ. We continue to welcome families from all backgrounds who seek to grow in their faith and serve God together.

"Passion for God, Compassion for People"

What Drives Us

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples who love Jesus, love each other, and love our community. We are committed to:

  • Worshipping God with all our hearts in both English and Bangla
  • Building strong, Christ-centered families
  • Equipping believers for ministry and service
  • Reaching our community with the Gospel of Jesus Christ
  • Celebrating our Bangladeshi heritage while embracing all cultures
  • Serving the needs of our local and global community

Our Vision

We envision a church where every person experiences the transforming love of Jesus Christ, where cultural diversity is celebrated, and where faith is lived out through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.
