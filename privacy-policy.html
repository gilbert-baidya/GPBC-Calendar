Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Grace and Praise Bangladeshi Church ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website gracepraise.church.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number when you submit prayer requests, register for events, or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to prayer requests and provide spiritual support
  • Send event notifications and church updates
  • Process donations securely
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate with you about church activities

3a. SMS Messaging Privacy

Grace and Praise Bangladeshi Church uses SMS messaging only to respond to inbound text messages that individuals voluntarily send to the church.

  • We do not send marketing or promotional text messages.
  • Message frequency varies based on user interaction.
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.
  • You may request help by replying HELP.
  • Standard message and data rates may apply.

Mobile information will not be shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Church leadership for prayer and pastoral care
  • Service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., payment processors)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of communications

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
